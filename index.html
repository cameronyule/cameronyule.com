About

I’m a technical director and programmer based in London.

I’ve planned, built and overseen many different types of projects, including websites, games, installations, mobile apps, ecommerce platforms, and technical operations and infrastructure.

I’m comfortable learning new technologies and techniques as required. Tech that I’ve used and enjoyed recently includes JavaScript, WebGL, Ruby, Docker, ClojureScript, Unity, C#, and RaspberryPi.

I’ll be available for contract and consultancy work from July 2016.

During this time I had the privilege of working with many high profile clients, including Disney, McDonald’s, BBC, Channel 4, British Museum, Science Museum, UK Parliament, Amplify, the Wellcome Collection, Tate Modern, Cartoon Network, and Adult Swim.

Please see Preloaded’s portfolio for examples of specific projects.

Prior to Preloaded, I held a variety of development roles at other creative agencies.
